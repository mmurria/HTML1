Ligers: Nature's feline unicorn

Where do Ligers come from?

Magic

Ligers are the offspring of mating between a female tiger and male lion.

This is not natural

Do they live in the wild?

Since lions and tigers live in two different regions of the world it has never been recorded of them mating in the wild. There are only about 100 Ligers in the world, all which are kept in zoos or natural reserves.

Where can I see a Liger?

You may recognize him from Netflixs' 'Tiger King', Doc Antle has a safari open to the public in Myrtle Beach, SC where you can visit his liger Hercules and many more wild animals.
